# Product Wiki Index

This wiki is the natural-language layer above the codebase.
It records what the product is meant to do, why decisions were made, and which behaviours must hold.

## Read first

- [Overview](overview.md): the whole-product map.
- [Log](log.md): the chronological record of wiki changes.

## Units

- [Actors](actors/): roles involved in the system.
- [Jobs](jobs/): durable user needs.
- [Stories](stories/): small product changes.
- [Acceptance criteria](acceptance-criteria/): observable done conditions.
- [Rules](rules/): product logic that must remain true.
- [Journeys](journeys/): end-to-end flows.
- [Capabilities](capabilities/): reusable product or system functions.
- [Outcomes](outcomes/): measures of success.
- [Non-goals](non-goals/): deliberate boundaries.
- [Assumptions](assumptions/): uncertain beliefs and risks.
- [Glossary](glossary/): shared language.
- [Decisions](decisions/): rationale and rejected options.
- [Design system](design-system/): product design and interface rules.

## Maintenance

Every non-trivial change should update this wiki before code changes.
Every acceptance criterion that matters should have an executable check.
